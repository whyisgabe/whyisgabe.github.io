Tener means "to have" in Spanish. To conjugate tener, please refer to the table below.

To correctly use tener in a sentance, you put the conjugated form, then "que", which means something on the lines of to, and lastly an action.
Ex. "Tengo que correr", which in English translates to I have to run.

Ir means "to go" in Spanish. To conjugate ir, please refer to the table below.

Conjugations of ir
Voy Vamos
Vas Vais
Va Van

To correctly use ir in a sentance, you put the conjugated form, then "a", which means to, and lastly a location.
Ex. "Voy a la playa", meaning I am going to the beach
